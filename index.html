What we do

SSVFD provides a range of emergency and community services in partnership with Warren County and neighboring agencies.

Fire suppression

Responding to structure fires, chimney fires, vehicle fires, and outdoor/brush fires throughout our first-due area and beyond.

Rescue & support

Assisting on motor-vehicle accidents, search and rescue, and medical incidents when requested by our partners.

Community coverage

Providing stand-bys for community events, fire safety checks, and public education opportunities.

Mutual aid

Working alongside neighboring departments to ensure the right resources reach every call.
